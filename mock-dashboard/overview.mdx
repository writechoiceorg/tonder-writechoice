## What is the Tonder Dashboard?

The Tonder Dashboard is your central hub for managing payments, viewing analytics, and configuring your account.

## Key Features

- **Transaction Management**: View and search all transactions
- **Analytics**: Monitor payment performance
- **API Keys**: Manage your credentials
- **Webhooks**: Configure notifications
- **Settlements**: Track payouts
- **Disputes**: Handle chargebacks
- **Reporting**: Export data and reports
