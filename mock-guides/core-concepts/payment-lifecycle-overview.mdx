## What is the Payment Lifecycle?

The payment lifecycle describes the complete journey of a payment from initiation to completion.

## Key Stages

1. **Initiation**: Customer starts the payment
2. **Authorization**: Payment is authorized by the bank
3. **Capture**: Funds are captured
4. **Settlement**: Money is transferred
5. **Reconciliation**: Transaction is reconciled
