[Placeholder for disputes documentation]

## What are Disputes?

Disputes occur when a customer challenges a payment with their card issuer.

## Common Dispute Reasons

- Product not received
- Product not as described
- Unauthorized transaction
- Duplicate charge
- Incorrect amount

## Dispute Process

1. **Customer files dispute**
2. **Notification received**
3. **Evidence collection**
4. **Response submission**
5. **Resolution**

## Handling Disputes

### Best Practices
- Respond quickly
- Provide clear evidence
- Maintain good records
- Communicate with customers

### Required Evidence
- Order details
- Delivery confirmation
- Customer communication
- Product description
- Transaction details
