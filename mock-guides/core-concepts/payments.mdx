[Placeholder for standard payments documentation]

## How Payments Work

Standard payment processing involves several steps to ensure secure and reliable transactions.

## Payment Flow

1. **Customer initiates payment**
2. **Payment details collected**
3. **Payment processed**
4. **Authorization received**
5. **Payment confirmed**

## Payment States

- **Pending**: Payment initiated
- **Authorized**: Bank approved
- **Captured**: Funds secured
- **Completed**: Transaction finished
- **Failed**: Payment unsuccessful
