## What is 3D Secure?

3D Secure (3DS) is an authentication protocol that adds an extra layer of security to card payments.

## How 3DS Works

1. **Payment initiated**
2. **3DS challenge triggered**
3. **Customer authenticates**
4. **Authentication verified**
5. **Payment processed**

## Benefits of 3DS

- Reduced fraud
- Liability shift to issuer
- Higher approval rates
- Enhanced security

## When to Use 3DS

- High-value transactions
- International payments
- Merchant preference
- Regulatory requirements
