The payment lifecycle describes the various stages a transaction goes through from the moment a customer makes a purchase to when the funds are settled in your bank account. Understanding this process is crucial for managing your finances, handling exceptions like refunds and disputes, and ensuring a smooth payment experience for your customers.

Tonder's platform provides full visibility into each stage of the lifecycle, ensuring transparency and accountability for every transaction.

## Core Stages of the Lifecycle
