## Payment Method Categories

### Card Payments
Traditional card processing with support for all major card networks. Best for immediate payment confirmation and worldwide acceptance.

**Available in:** All markets  
**Processing Time:** Immediate  
**Best For:** E-commerce, subscriptions, recurring payments

### Cash Payments
Allow customers to pay in cash at convenience stores and payment centers. Popular with unbanked customers.

**Available in:** Mexico, Brazil, Colombia, Peru, Chile  
**Processing Time:** 24-48 hours  
**Best For:** Customers without bank accounts, high-trust purchases

### Bank Transfers
Direct bank-to-bank transfers using local payment rails. Secure and widely trusted.

**Available in:** Mexico (SPEI), Brazil (PIX, TED), Colombia (PSE)  
**Processing Time:** Minutes to hours  
**Best For:** Large transactions, B2B payments

### Digital Wallets
E-wallets and digital payment platforms with high adoption in Latin America.

**Available in:** All markets  
**Processing Time:** Immediate  
**Best For:** Users with existing wallet accounts, one-click checkout
